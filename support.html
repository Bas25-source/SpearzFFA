Frequently Asked Questions
How do I report a hacker? +
Join our Discord and use the #report-a-player channel. Include their username and any evidence (screenshots, video).
I bought something in the store but didn't receive it. +
Make sure you're online on the server. Items are delivered in-game. If it still doesn't work, contact a staff member on Discord with proof of purchase.
Can I apply to become a staff member? +
Yes! Staff applications are open occasionally. Check #announcements in our Discord for when applications open.
What versions are supported? +
Spearz FFA supports Minecraft Java Edition versions 1.8 through 1.20.x. We recommend 1.8 for the best PvP experience.
How do I appeal a ban? +
Join our Discord and open a ticket in the #ban-appeals channel. Explain your situation and a staff member will review your case.
